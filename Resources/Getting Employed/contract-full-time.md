title: Contract vs Full Time
---
content:

Contract:
- 1099
- own expenses (can write off)
- accounting
- have to ask for higher rate
- higher taxes (take 15% off income for self-employment tax)
- varied projects
- varied employers
- possible varied hours


Full-time:
- benefits
- paid expenses
- stable employement
- 40 hours a week
